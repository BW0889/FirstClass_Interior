Offices

Not only have we done amazing jobs in retail and hospitality, but also in commercial businesses and their offices! We have had the privilege of working with a couple of offices in Christchurch to deliver these efficient designs. Starting with the left image, we have a gorgeous open-plan work space with natural earthy colours. With the large windows and a nice open plan, employees will not feel cooped up in tiny desk spaces. In the middle is a smaller open plan with offices off to the side, creating a workspace that can accommodate any work style. Social workers can work alongside or across from other employees, while more introverted or quiet workers can tuck away into an office for some alone time. And finally, to the right is a board room with lovely light tones and a complementary colour pallet. These workspaces are perfect for making your employees excited to come to work.

Homes

We have had the pleasure of working in many beautiful homes over our time in the industry. Some of our recent projects include kitchens, bathrooms, living rooms, and bedrooms. On the left is one of our most recent living rooms furnished with a gorgeous light grey-dull blue colour pallet which really compliments the rest of the room. In the center is our new kitchen/dining room with a lovely hardwood frame, hardwood table and chairs, and hardwood cabinetry. It gives the room a rustic but natural feel. When paired with the dark grey island and stools, it brings it all together as an amazing room! Finally, to the right is another one of our recent living rooms. Similar to the living room on the left, this one has light-coloured tones which pair perfectly with the north-facing window, allowing for sunlight to stream through and light up the whole space.
